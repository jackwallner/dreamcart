DreamCart

Shopping, but make it free. Browse fashion, fill your cart, check out with coins — the dopamine hit with zero buyer's remorse.

Virtual shopping game

DreamCart is a fashion shopping game for iPhone. Every item, coin, and delivery is imaginary and has no cash value. Optional coin packs let you shop more, but you never have to spend real money to play.

Window shop without guilt Add to cart, checkout, unbox, repeat. Pieces land in your closet with a little celebration.
Free coins every day Claim your daily drop, keep your streak alive, and spin the wheel for bonus coins.
Style your dream looks Mix brands into full outfits — tops, bottoms, shoes, jackets, jewelry, and accessories.
