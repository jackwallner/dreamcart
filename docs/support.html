DreamCart is a virtual fashion shopping game. You browse AI-generated style pieces, buy them with in-app coins, and build a closet and outfits. Nothing in the app is real merchandise, and coins have no cash value.

Getting started

  • Sign in with Apple to save your closet, coins, and streak across devices.
  • Claim your daily coin drop on the Today tab.
  • Spin the wheel once per day for a chance at bonus coins.
  • Browse the shop, add items to your cart, and check out with coins.

Coin packs & purchases

DreamCart offers optional consumable coin packs ($1.99–$49.99). All payments are handled by Apple. Coins can only be spent inside DreamCart and cannot be refunded as cash.

  • To restore purchases after reinstalling, open the coin shop and tap Restore Purchases.
  • For billing questions or refunds, contact Apple Support — we cannot process refunds directly.

Troubleshooting

  • Coins didn't appear after a purchase — tap Restore Purchases in the coin shop, then force-quit and reopen the app while online.
  • Daily drop or spin unavailable — each resets once per calendar day (UTC). Make sure you're signed in.
  • Images not loading — check your internet connection; product photos load on demand.
  • Can't sign in — confirm Sign in with Apple is enabled for your Apple ID in Settings.

Delete your account

Open the You tab → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, closet, coins, and streak from our servers.
